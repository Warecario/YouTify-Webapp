# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 20, 2026

Welcome to **YouTfiy-Webapp** ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how your information is handled when you use our web application hosted at [https://warecario.github.io/YouTfiy-Webapp/](https://warecario.github.io/YouTfiy-Webapp/).

## 1. Information We Collect and Process
YouTfiy-Webapp operates as a client-side application running directly in your browser to provide a customized listening experience by bridging metadata and audio services. We prioritize your privacy through minimal data collection:
* **No Personal Server Storage:** We do not collect, store, or process your personal data, listening history, or search queries on external servers or databases operated by us.
* **Local Storage:** The application may use your browser's local storage (such as `localStorage` or `sessionStorage`) to save your preferences, playlist configurations, or session tokens locally on your device. This data stays on your machine.
* **Spotify Metadata and YouTube Audio:** The core functionality of YouTfiy-Webapp involves organizing Spotify metadata and utilizing YouTube's audio engine. Data exchanged with these services (such as track information, search requests, and audio streams) is processed directly between your active browser session and the respective APIs. We do not log, retain, or intercept this data.

## 2. Third-Party Services and APIs
Because YouTfiy-Webapp relies on external services to function, your usage of those specific features is subject to their respective privacy policies:
* **Spotify:** The retrieval of track metadata and playlist information is governed by the [Spotify Privacy Policy](https://www.spotify.com/us/legal/privacy-policy/).
* **YouTube / Google:** The utilization of the audio engine and related media services is governed by the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
* **GitHub Pages:** This application is hosted on GitHub Pages, which may automatically collect standard technical logs (such as IP addresses and browser user agent details) as part of providing the infrastructure. Please review the [GitHub Privacy Statement](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement) for more details.

## 3. How We Use Information
Any processing occurs locally on your device or via direct client-side requests strictly for the following purposes:
* To integrate metadata and audio sources to deliver the application's intended listening features.
* To remember your interface preferences and user settings across sessions via local storage.

## 4. Data Security
We employ standard safeguards appropriate for a client-side web application. However, because data may be stored locally in your browser or communicated directly with third-party APIs, we cannot guarantee absolute security against unauthorized local access to your device or external network risks.

## 5. Children’s Privacy
YouTfiy-Webapp does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any modifications will be posted directly on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or the application, please feel free to reach out via our GitHub repository or project page.
